Lower Prosthetics

Amal Company provides all types of prosthetic limbs for lower amputations - the best imported prosthetic limbs - the latest European prosthetic limbs from Germany, England, America, France, Australia, Spain, Holland.

Upper Prosthetics

Amal company provides all types of prosthetic limbs for upper amputations - the best imported prosthetic limbs - the latest European prosthetic limbs from Germany, England, America, France, Australia, Spain, Holland.

Physical Rehabilitation

Amal company provides all kinds of physical rehabilitation to the patient through specialized doctors, which includes rehabilitating the patient on how to use the prosthesis, how to install and remove it, and how it works.

Psychological Rehabilitation

Amal company provides a selection of the best psychiatrists at the global level who work on rehabilitating the mentally ill to accept the prosthetic limb and coexist with it.
