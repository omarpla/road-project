About Us



Road Company

The cycles for replacing phones, computers, and electronic devices are getting shorter than before, as the class difference in societies has increased. Our job is to take old and used appliances from the rich and middle class to workshops to fix the fixable and recycle the irreparable. then we sell it at a very low price to those in need quickly and easily and with the least losses and cost

Vision

We at ROAD strive to be the best and first in providing hardware around the world.

Our Mission

Our goal is to help the poor and refugees to equip the grooms who are unable to buy expensive appliances, and to equip homes for them. And reduce the waste of electronic devices
